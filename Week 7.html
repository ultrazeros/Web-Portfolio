Week 7 Networks of Agency

Pick an activity that uses technology (such as tracking the stock market or driving to the movies) and create a map of the actors and interactions involved (human, technological, social, cultural, environmental, etc.). Find and an image or video of the task being done to help prompt your analysis and mapping. Be guided by the principles of situated action, the extended mind, and actor-network theory in undertaking this mapping process.


The task for Week 7 is to pick a task that involves the use of technology and create a map of actors and interaction. As for the chosen task it is the agents and interaction involved in going to Gold Coast Griffith University Campus.


“Actors come together to form temporary networks anchored by another actor, creating assemblages of relations specific to an individual act or broader event and forming a collective (an “actant”)” – Slide 8 Liza Potts.
